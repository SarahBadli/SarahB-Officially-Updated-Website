My Education

PRIMARY SCHOOL

SEKOLAH KEMBANGSAAN AMPANG (SKA)

From 2009 until 2014, I was a student at SK Ampang, my first elementary school. When I was in school, I was really involved with the sports club. I was renowned as a sportswoman who frequently competed at a high level on behalf of my school. I competed in a number of events and won first place in the high jump and the running event. My athletic abilities were greatly enhanced by SK Ampang. There, I formed lifelong friendships with professors and students and produced numerous memories that will last a lifetime.

ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

AS SOFA CENTER OF EXCELLENT (ACEL)

In the years 2015 and 2019 , attending Sofa, a private and religious school, was among my most significant experiences. My time there was invaluable, particularly in the field of religious studies, for my education. Now that my 30-juz "hafazan" is over, I am quite grateful. On top of that, I passed the SPM after spending a year in an accelerated program at As Sofa's Central of Excellence. Everything that has happened to me, both good and bad, has made me who I am today, and for that I am eternally thankful.

UINIVERSITY

UNIVERSITI TEKNOLOGI MARA (UiTM)

when in 2022 I was offered to enter uitm and my family and I accepted the offer. I gained a lot from my time at UiTM. The information management skills and knowledge I gained from taking IM110 have been priceless. Participating enthusiastically in extracurricular activities and academic endeavors aided my professional and personal development. I was able to attain academic distinction and make lasting connections because to the university's supportive environment and committed teachers. When I look back on my professional trajectory and the steps I took to get here, I can see how important UiTM was.
